## Symbolism Analysis

### Patriot Front Flag
The primary Patriot Front flag is designed to blend American aesthetics with explicit fascist symbolism:

- **Layout:** Blue chevron at hoist with alternating white and red sections toward the fly
- **Central emblem:** A **fasces** (bundle of sticks with axe — the symbol of fascism, from which the word "fascism" derives) between two circular segments
- **Surrounding:** Ring of **13 stars** — representing the original 13 colonies, invoking a "pre-diversity" America
- **Colors:** Red, white, and blue — deliberately mimicking American flag colors

    The flag brahn described — "stormfront logo where the stars are supposed to be on the American flag" — matches the Patriot Front flag design. The fasces + 13 stars replaces the 50-star canton of the US flag. The overall effect is an American flag with fascism literally substituted for the states. **This is by design.** The group calls their ideology "American Fascism."

- **Uniforms** — blue jackets, khaki pants, white face masks: paramilitary aesthetic while hiding identities
- **U-Haul trucks** — logistical signature; used for transport, staging, and rapid exit

### Historical Lineage of Symbols
The fasces is:
- An ancient Roman symbol of authority (bundle of rods surrounding an axe)
- Adopted by Mussolini's Italian Fascist Party (1920s-1940s)
- The literal origin of the word "fascism"
- Now used by Patriot Front as their central emblem
- Deliberately chosen as more "publicly acceptable" than the swastika while carrying the same ideological weight
