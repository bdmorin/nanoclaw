## Tactics

### Recruitment
- **Fitness clubs** — "active clubs" serve as recruitment fronts targeting young men seeking community

## Violence and Legal History

### Charlottesville Connection (2017)
- Vanguard America (Patriot Front's predecessor) was present at Unite the Right
- Rousseau personally led VA members
- James Alex Fields Jr. marched with VA before murdering Heather Heyer
- Patriot Front was founded in the direct aftermath as a "rebrand"

### Boston Attack (2022)
- July 2, 2022: Patriot Front members attacked Charles M. Murrel III (Black man) during a march in Boston
- **January 13, 2025:** Federal judge ordered Patriot Front to pay **$2.7 million** ($755K damages + $2M punitive)
- No individual criminally charged

### Idaho Arrest (2022)
- June 11, 2022: 31 Patriot Front members arrested near a Pride event in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho
- Found in back of U-Haul with shields, shin guards, and a smoke grenade
- Charged with conspiracy to riot
- Included members from Texas, including Rousseau

### Other Documented Violence
- Multiple incidents of vandalism, intimidation, property destruction
- Pattern of confrontation at public events
- Threats to racial, ethnic, and religious minorities

## Intelligence Assessment

1. Explicit fascist ideology with political infiltration strategy

3. Recurring public demonstrations with paramilitary aesthetics
