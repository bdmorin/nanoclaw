## OVERALL ANALYSIS

Report presents well-documented facts about incident, victim background, and official investigations with strong corroboration across independent sources. Critical weakness: central disputed claim (whether Pretti was armed) remains genuinely unresolved, with competing narratives unsupported by publicly available evidence. Source quality inconsistency (mixing news organizations with Know Your Meme) undermines credibility. Recommendation: Accept verified biographical and procedural facts as reliable; remain agnostic on weapon possession pending full evidence release; recognize legitimate gaps in public information prevent definitive conclusions about force justification.
