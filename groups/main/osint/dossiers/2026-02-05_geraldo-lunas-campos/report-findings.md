## Executive Summary

On January 3, 2026, 55-year-old Geraldo Lunas Campos died at Camp East Montana ICE detention facility in El Paso, Texas. The El Paso County Coroner ruled his death a **HOMICIDE** caused by asphyxia due to neck and torso compression. Witnesses report guards handcuffed, tackled, and choked him until unconscious while he said "I can't breathe" in Spanish. ICE claims he was attempting suicide. This is the first death in 2026, occurring 4 days before Renée Good's shooting.
