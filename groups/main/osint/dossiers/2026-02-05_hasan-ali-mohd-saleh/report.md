## Executive Summary

On October 11, 2025, Hasan Ali Moh'D Saleh, a 67-year-old Jordanian national, died at Larkin Community Hospital in Miami, Florida after suffering cardiac arrest while in ICE custody. Saleh had a significant medical history including hypertension, heart disease, renal disease, and diabetes. He was taken to the hospital on October 10 due to fever, admitted for treatment, and suffered cardiac arrest on October 11 at approximately 6:32 PM. Despite resuscitation attempts, he died at 7:13 PM. Saleh had lived in the United States for over 31 years after entering legally in 1994, but was arrested by ICE in September 2025 pursuant to a final removal order and detained at Krome Detention Center. His death marked the 17th ICE custody death in 2025, part of the deadliest year for ICE detainees in two decades.
