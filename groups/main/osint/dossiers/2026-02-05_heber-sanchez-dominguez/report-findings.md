## Executive Summary

On January 14, 2026, Heber Sanchez Dominguez, a 34-year-old Mexican national, husband and father of two, was found dead in his cell at Robert A. Deyton Detention Facility in Clayton County, Georgia. ICE claims "apparent suicide" - found "hanging by the neck." He had been in custody for 7 days. Death occurred at 2:05 AM. Rep. David Scott is demanding transparency and accountability. No cause of death officially determined yet. Fifth death in ICE custody in first 16 days of 2026.
