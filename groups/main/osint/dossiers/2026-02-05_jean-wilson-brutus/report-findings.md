## Executive Summary

On December 12, 2025, Jean Wilson Brutus, a 41-year-old Haitian man, died at University Hospital in Newark, New Jersey, just 24 HOURS after entering ICE custody at Delaney Hall Detention Facility. He was arrested December 11 for criminal mischief (property damage) and transferred directly from Union County Jail to ICE detention. During intake, he showed "no signs of distress" and had "no medical history of cardiovascular issues." Within 24 hours, he experienced a medical emergency, was transported to University Hospital, and died December 12. ICE claims "suspected natural causes" but the official autopsy was "inconclusive." His family hired an independent firm for a second autopsy, stating: "We need another autopsy because we need clarity on what happened." This is the fourth death in December 2025, and the first of four deaths within a FOUR-DAY SPAN (Dec 12-15). His death sparked calls to close Delaney Hall and demands from Senator Cory Booker for a "clear accounting of what happened in this tragedy."

🔴 **Critical Issues:**

1. **24-Hour Death:** Healthy 41-year-old dead within 24 hours of entering custody

2. **Inconclusive Autopsy:** Official autopsy cannot determine cause of death - highly suspicious

3. **Family Seeking Answers:** Independent autopsy requested; family doesn't trust official findings

4. **No Transparency:** No disclosure of what medical emergency occurred or what happened in custody

5. **Delaney Hall History:** Facility has long history of complaints; Senator Booker calls for closure

6. **Four Deaths in Four Days:** Part of unprecedented cluster (Dec 12-15)

7. **Humanitarian Parole to Death:** Legal entrant under Biden program dead 24 hours after ICE takes custody

**Assessment:** Jean Wilson Brutus's death is one of the most suspicious in December 2025's deadly month. A healthy 41-year-old with no cardiovascular history, cleared at intake with "no signs of distress," dies within 24 hours of entering ICE custody. The official autopsy is "inconclusive" - meaning medical examiners cannot determine what killed him. This is extraordinary. Natural deaths (heart attack, stroke, aneurysm) have clear autopsy findings. "Inconclusive" suggests either: (1) the cause is not natural and unclear, (2) trauma occurred but cannot be definitively linked to death, or (3) something is being concealed. The family's decision to seek independent autopsy indicates they do not trust the official findings. Senator Booker's statement - demanding "a clear accounting" and facility closure - reflects the gravity of this death. Without video footage, full autopsy transparency, and independent investigation, Jean Wilson Brutus's death will remain a symbol of ICE's deadly lack of accountability.

**Recommendation:** Immediate release of video surveillance footage, complete transparency in autopsy findings, independent federal investigation, disclosure of what happened in the 24 hours between intake and death, closure of Delaney Hall pending investigation, and accountability for all staff involved in his custody.
