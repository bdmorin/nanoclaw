## Executive Summary

On October 29, 2025, Jose Garcia-Sorto, a Honduran national, was shot by an ICE agent during a traffic stop on Interstate 17 in north Phoenix, Arizona at approximately 4 AM. DHS claims Garcia-Sorto began pulling away and an agent in the path of the vehicle fired "defensively." Garcia-Sorto was hospitalized in stable condition. The FBI is investigating.
