## Executive Summary

On December 31, 2025 (New Year's Eve), Keith Porter Jr., a 43-year-old Black man, father of two, was shot and killed by his neighbor - an off-duty ICE agent identified as **Brian Palacios** - at their apartment complex in Northridge, Los Angeles. Porter had been firing celebratory gunshots into the air to ring in the new year.

**Critical findings:**
- Agent Palacios has been accused in court filings of **child abuse** (whipping sons with belt), **racist remarks** about Black and Latino people, and **homophobic slurs**
- Some neighbors say they did NOT hear the agent identify himself as law enforcement
- No charges have been filed against Palacios
- Porter's family and attorneys Ben Crump and Jamal Tooson are calling on California AG Rob Bonta to investigate
- LAPD's Justice System Integrity Division is investigating

## 2. INCIDENT TIMELINE

### December 31, 2025 - New Year's Eve

**Time:** Shortly after 10:30 PM
**Location:** Apartment complex, Northridge, Los Angeles

**Context:**
- New Year's Eve celebration
- Porter firing recently acquired gun into the air (celebratory)
- Witness observed gunfire in three-shot intervals with ~1 minute between volleys

**DHS Official Account:**

1. Off-duty ICE agent Brian Palacios heard gunshots near apartment
2. Retrieved his ICE-authorized firearm
3. Left apartment to investigate
4. Encountered Porter holding a long rifle (AR-15-style)
5. Agent identified himself as law enforcement
6. Porter allegedly pointed weapon at agent
7. Agent ordered Porter to put down weapon
8. Porter refused to comply
9. Agent fired "defensively" with service weapon

**Disputed Elements:**

- **Neighbors say they did NOT hear agent identify himself**
- Family/advocates say Porter was simply celebrating the holiday
- Advocates argue off-duty agent should have called police and waited

**Outcome:**
- Porter shot and killed
- Discovered dead in apartment complex courtyard

---

## 3. THE AGENT: BRIAN PALACIOS

**Identification:**
- Name revealed through court documents in custody dispute
- Ex-husband of Palacios' current partner filed documents
- Lives in same apartment complex where shooting occurred

**Agency:** ICE Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO)

**Allegations in Court Filings:**

1. **Child Abuse:**
   - LA County judge barred Palacios from being around girlfriend's children
   - Accused of whipping his biological sons with a belt

2. **Racist Remarks:**
   - Made racist remarks about Black and Latino people (per children's attorney)
   - Called children's biological father an "illegal alien"

3. **Homophobic Slurs:**
   - Used homophobic slurs (per children's attorney)

4. **Gun at Youth Event:**
   - Allegedly carried a gun at a youth soccer game

**Agent's Defense:**
- Attorney Stacie Halpern claims self-defense
- Denies racist remarks
- Cites DCFS and LAPD reports deeming child abuse allegations "unfounded"
