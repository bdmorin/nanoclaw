## Executive Summary

On January 6, 2026, Luis Beltran Yanez-Cruz, a 68-year-old Honduran grandfather who had lived in the U.S. for decades, died at JFK Memorial Hospital in Indio, California from heart-related issues. He had been in ICE custody for 51 days after being arrested in New Jersey and transferred across the country to California. Family reports he complained of illness for weeks but received only "Band-Aid solutions" at the detention facility. He died 26 hours after being helicoptered to hospital with chest pain. Third death of 2026, one day after Luis Gustavo Nunez Caceres.
