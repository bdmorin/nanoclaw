## Executive Summary

On January 5, 2026, Luis Gustavo Nunez Caceres, a 42-year-old Honduran national, died at HCA Houston Healthcare in Conroe, Texas after being admitted for "chronic heart-related health issues." He had been in ICE custody for 49 days (arrested Nov 17, 2025). ICE claims comprehensive medical care was provided. No criminal record. Second death of 2026, occurring 2 days after Geraldo Lunas Campos's homicide.

## ICE CLAIM ANALYSIS

**ICE States:**
> "Comprehensive medical care was provided throughout his detention, including health screenings and access to medical appointments"

**Red Flags:**
- Vague language ("comprehensive," "access to")
- No specifics on what care was actually given
- "Access to" ≠ "received" medical care
- No timeline of medical interventions
- No disclosure of specific diagnosis

**Pattern:**
- Similar language used in other detention deaths
- "Heart-related issues" common official cause
- Often no independent verification
- Medical records rarely released publicly
