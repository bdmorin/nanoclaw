## Executive Summary

On April 16, 2025, Nhon Ngoc Nguyen, a 55-58-year-old Vietnamese refugee suffering from dementia, died at the Long Term Acute Care Hospital in El Paso, Texas, while in ICE custody. He was arrested during a routine ICE check-in on February 24, 2025, in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Family alleges medical neglect, citing inability to locate him for weeks and concerns about inadequate care for his cognitive impairment. Autopsy confirmed death by acute pneumonia with dementia as secondary cause.
