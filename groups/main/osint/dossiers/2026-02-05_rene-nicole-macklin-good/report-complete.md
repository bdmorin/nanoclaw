## Executive Summary

On January 7, 2026, Renée Nicole Macklin Good, a 37-year-old American woman and mother of three, was fatally shot by ICE agent Jonathan Ross in Minneapolis during Operation Metro Surge. Good had stopped to support neighbors when she was shot three times. Her death was ruled a homicide. No charges filed against Ross. Video evidence contradicts federal self-defense claims.

**Renée Nicole Macklin Good**

- Married
- Loved singing and poetry
- Had just dropped son at school

## THE SHOOTING - January 7, 2026

**Shooter:** ICE Agent Jonathan Ross
**Location:** South Minneapolis residential street
**Operation:** Metro Surge (2,000 agents deployed Jan 6)

**Sequence:**
1. Good's car stopped sideways in street
2. Ross walks around vehicle
3. Agents order her to exit, reaching through window
4. Good briefly reverses, then moves forward/right
5. Ross at front-left fires 3 shots as she passes (turning AWAY from him)
   - Shot 1: Windshield
   - Shot 2: Through open window
   - Shot 3: Fatal temple shot
