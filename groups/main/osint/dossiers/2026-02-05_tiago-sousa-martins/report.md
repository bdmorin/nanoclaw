## Executive Summary

On December 24, 2025 (Christmas Eve), Tiago Alexandre Sousa-Martins, a Portuguese immigrant, was shot by ICE ERO agents during an attempted arrest in Glen Burnie, Maryland. He was shot in the shoulder and left thigh, resulting in a collapsed lung and hospitalization. DHS initially claimed he tried to run over officers; local police contradicted this account. An El Salvadoran passenger was also injured. The case drew congressional attention, with Senator Van Hollen claiming ICE has been given a "license to kill."
