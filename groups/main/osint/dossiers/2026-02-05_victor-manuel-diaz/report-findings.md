## Executive Summary

On January 14, 2026, Victor Manuel Diaz, a 36-year-old Nicaraguan national, died at Camp East Montana in El Paso, Texas. He was arrested January 6 in Minneapolis during Operation Metro Surge and died 8 days later. ICE claims "presumed suicide" but his family strongly disputes this: "I don't believe he took his life." This is the THIRD death at Camp East Montana in 12 days (after Geraldo Lunas Campos's homicide on Jan 3). Critically, ICE bypassed the El Paso medical examiner for the autopsy, sending it to Army medical center at Fort Bliss instead - breaking with protocol used for previous Camp East Montana deaths.
