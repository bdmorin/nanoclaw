## Executive Summary

On January 14, 2026 at approximately 6:50 PM CT, ICE agents shot Julio Cesar Sosa-Celis, a 24-year-old Venezuelan man, in the leg during an attempted arrest in north Minneapolis. The shooting occurred due to **mistaken identity** — the agents were targeting a different person entirely. Sosa-Celis's partner told 911 that agents fired through the front door of their home. Photographic evidence of a bullet hole in the front door, presented at a court hearing on February 3, 2026, confirmed the family's account.

The aftermath included ICE tear-gassing a residential neighborhood, sending a 6-month-old baby and two young children to the hospital. Sosa-Celis and Alfredo Aljorna were charged with assaulting a federal officer, but a judge ordered them released — ICE then immediately re-detained them.

**Key finding:** This shooting resulted from mistaken identity by out-of-state ICE agents unfamiliar with Minnesota. The agents ran plates on a car purchased off Facebook Marketplace for $750 and assumed the driver was someone else.

## 2. INCIDENT TIMELINE

### Pre-Incident
- Two out-of-state ICE agents, unfamiliar with Minnesota, ran license plates on a vehicle
- Database showed the car registered to **Joffre Barrera**, a 35-year-old flagged as undocumented
- The actual driver was **Alfredo Alejandro Aljorna**, who had purchased the car off Facebook Marketplace for $750
- **Neither the driver nor Sosa-Celis was the person the agents were looking for**

### The Shooting — January 14, 2026, ~6:50 PM CT
1. ICE agents attempted a traffic stop on the vehicle
2. Vehicle fled, crashed into a parked car
3. Driver fled on foot, agents pursued
4. ICE claims: Struggle on ground, two people from nearby apartment attacked agent with broom and snow shovel
5. **Sosa-Celis's partner's account:** Agents fired through the front door, striking Sosa-Celis inside the home
6. **February 3 court hearing:** Photographic evidence of bullet hole in front door confirmed family's account
7. Sosa-Celis shot in the leg
8. Both the agent and Sosa-Celis hospitalized

### Aftermath
- ICE agents entered home, arrested Sosa-Celis, Aljorna, and a third person
- Community protest erupted, lasting past 2:00 AM
- **Police deployed tear gas in the residential neighborhood**
- A family's 6-month-old baby and two young children were rushed to hospital due to tear gas exposure
- Home at shooting scene later boarded up

### FBI Investigation Findings

- Agents were out-of-state, unfamiliar with Minnesota
- License plate lookup produced wrong person
- Car had been sold via Facebook Marketplace

### Key Evidence
- **Bullet hole in front door** — photographic evidence presented at Feb 3 court hearing
- This confirms the family's account that agents fired through/at the door
- DHS did not address this evidence in public statements
