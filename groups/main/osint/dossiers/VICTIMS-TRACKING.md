## Historical Context (2010-2026)

- **400+ documented deaths** from ICE/CBP/Border Patrol operations
- **59 shootings by ICE** (2015-2021) across 26 states → 23 fatalities
- **Zero indictments** of ICE agents for shootings (The Trace, 2021)

**Most common causes:**
1. Vehicle crashes
2. Medical issues (often denied care)
3. Shootings
4. Falls from barriers
5. Unknown causes
6. Drownings

**Geographic concentration:** Texas accounts for ~40% of all deaths

### Critical Systemic Finding
- **Oct 3, 2025:** ICE halted payments to contractors providing medical care in detention facilities
- Payments unlikely to resume until end of April 2026
- This occurred during record-breaking detention population growth
- **Correlation:** Dec 2025 was deadliest month on record (7 deaths), coinciding with 2+ months of halted medical payments
