## Key Patterns

1. **Zero accountability:** No criminal charges against any federal agent for any shooting or death
2. **Medical neglect is systemic:** 11+ deaths involved documented denial or delay of care
3. **"Suicide" claims need scrutiny:** 5 deaths classified as suicide — independent verification unavailable
4. **Texas concentration:** ~40% of all deaths in Texas facilities
5. **Private operators:** GEO Group and CoreCivic facilities disproportionately represented
6. **Cover-ups documented:** Brown (HSI told police to omit shooting), Parias (body cam contradicts claims), Sousa-Martins (DHS narrative contradicted by local police)
7. **December 2025 unprecedented:** 7 deaths in 31 days, 4 deaths in 4 days (Dec 12-15)
8. **US citizens killed:** Good and Pretti — both shot by federal agents in Minneapolis
